Welcome to Your Smart Desktop

A smart desktop is the hub of all activities. It contains all of your mission critical applications and contains features that allow you to work faster and smarter. Click Next to learn more about the tools at your fingertips.

Launch your applications

Your mission critical applications can be launched from your toolbar at the top of your screen. Simply select the Apps menu to view the applications that are available to you. Use the star icon to favorite applications for faster access in the future.

Snap applications together and adjust borders

Create intuitive, custom arrangements of applications by simply moving two or more windows together. When applications are snapped like this, their borders are shared and can be dragged to adjust both windows.

Attach apps together for easy arrangement

Once you have created a custom arrangement of windows, attach the windows together by clicking on the "Attach" icon on any window in the group. Now application windows will move as a single unit.

Use Workspaces to keep your apps task-centric

Mission critical applications can be launched, snapped, and attached together to create your very custom desktop. Experiment with different layouts and configurations. Once you have found the right fit, save and name your layout as a workspace to use in the future.
